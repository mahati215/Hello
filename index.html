Cats and Dogs

Cats

• Cats have flexible bodies and teeth that they use for hunting small animals such as mice and rats.
• A group of cats is called clowder
• Cats are on of the most popular pets in the world
• Cats have a great sense of sense of smell
• Cats live and average of 12-15 years

Fun Facts

• There is an estimated 200-500 million cats alive om Earth
• There may be domesticated cats as early as 7,500 B.C. which was 9,500 years ago!
• The heaviest cat ever weighed 50 pounds!

Dogs

• Dogs are a direct descendant of wolves
• Puppies sleep around 18-20 years
• When puppies are born they are blind, deaf, and toothless
• Adult dogs have 42 teeth and puppies have 28 teeth
• There are around 340 breeds of dogs
• Dogs live an average of 10-14 years

Fun Facts

• Dogs use their whiskers to help them see in the dark
• A dogs sweat glands are on their paws
• Dogs can hear four times better than humans
• Dogs can smell people's feelings
• More than half of U.S. presidents have owned dogs
